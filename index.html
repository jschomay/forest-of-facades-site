Forest of Facades

Fight battles of deduction and deception

An asymmetric competitive puzzle game • 2-player • 10-minute • Age 8+

Monsters have emerged from the forest to attack your village! You call on heroes to defend. There's just one problem -- only one player knows each card's strength! One player has to deduce the hidden strengths from the outcomes of previous battles before time runs out, and the other has to reveal as little information as possible long enough to destroy the whole village.
